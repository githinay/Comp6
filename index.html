What is Global Warming?

Global warming is a problem for humans and other living organisms.

Global warming refers to the increase of temperature because of several things.

Global warming may disrupt ecosystems, and it can cause extreme weather events like hurricanes.

Global warming is a significant and rapidly accelerating issue caused by human activities.

This problem can cause massive destruction to the environment.


Possible Causes of Global Warming

The list below are some of the possible causes of Global Warming.

  • Deforestation
  • Poor Waste Management
  • Vehicles
  • Industries or Factories
  • Use of Synthetic Chemicals

Possible Effects of Global Warming

The list below are some of the possible effects of Global Warming.

  • Extremely High Temperature
  • Decrease of Products
  • Extinction of some Living Organisms
  • Erosions
  • Destruction of Nature

How to Prevent Global Warming

The list below are some ways to prevent global warming.

  • Manage Wastes Properly
  • Use Eco-Friendly Materials
  • Keep the environment clean
  • Plant more trees
  • Prevent the Use of Dangerous Chemicals

We must prevent global warming as it is very dangerous for us humans and other living organisms. We can save our environment by stopping global warming.
